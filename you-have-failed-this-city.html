'Arrow' Quotes: "You Have Failed This City"

  • Robert King

    Season 1, Episdoe 1

    Oliver's dad kicked the whole "failed this city" thing off on their life boat, when he told his son: "I'm not the man you think I am. I didn't build our city. I failed it. And I wasn't the only one."

  • Marcus Redman

    Season 1, Episode 2

    What he did: Stole $30 million from pensioners.

    What happened to him: Oliver made him return the money. 

  • Leo Mueller

    Season 1, Episode 5

    What he did: Dealt in stolen weapons.

    What happened to him: Oliver killed him.   

  • Anthony Venza

    Season 1, Episode 8

    Note: Oliver didn't speak these words. The Huntress did.

    What he did: Dealt drugs, and had the misfortune of being Oliver's first joint takedown with Helena Bertinelli.

    What Happened to him: He was arrested, which was about the best outcome he would have hoped for (Helena had a habit of killing her prey).

  • Oliver Queen

    Season 1, Episode 9

    Note: A dejected Oliver told this to Diggle: "When I confront somebody on the list, I tell them that they failed the city. But tonight, it was me who failed."

    What he did: Oliver felt guilty for nearly losing a fight with the Dark Archer and letting him live.

    What happened to him: Oliver took some time off from being The Hood.

  • Moira Queen

    Season 1, Episode 13

    What she did: Moira was involved in Robert Queen's death and in The Undertaking (though Oliver didn't know it yet.)

    What happened to her: Nothing…right away. Shortly after Oliver uttered his famous words, she shot him with a pistol. In episode 23, she ultimately atoned for her sins and admitted during as speech that she had failed the city. 

  • Guillermo Barrera

    Season 1, Episode 16

    What he did: Agreed to kill Malcolm Merlyn for money.

    What happened to him: Oliver killed him with an arrow.

  • Count Vertigo

    Season 1, Episode 19

    Sometimes, people turn Oliver's words back on him. In this case, an institutionalized Count Vertigo screams "you have failed this city" after receiving a visit from Oliver. 

  • Harold Backman

    Season 1, Episode 21

    What he did: Backman helped steal millions as an accountant for a criminal organization.

    What happened to him: Oliver let him live, but took his laptop.

  • Malcolm Merlyn

    Season 1, Episode 22

    What he did: Plotted the undertaking, which ultimately killed 502 people in The Glades.

    What happened to him: He and Oliver duked it out — with Merlyn defeating him and learning his secret identity. 

  • Mayor Altman

    Season 2, Episode 1

    Note: Oliver did not say the famous words. A group of copycats called The Hoods did.

    What he did: The Mayor failed to protect the city from the Undertaking, though come on. Who could have predicted that?

    What happened to him: The mayor was assassinated by The Hoods during a speech in which he was apologizing for The Undertaking. 

  • Matin Somers

    Season 1, Episode 2

    What he did: Somers was a bad dude and was involved in the Chinese Triad's drug trade.

    What happened to him: Oliver suspended him upside down until he confessed to his crimes. He was ultimately arrested. 

  • Oliver Queen

    Note: Oliver said this about himself, telling Felicity "I have failed this city."

    Season 2, Episode 22

    What he did: Let Starling City be torn apart by Slade Wilson and his mirakuru -crazed criminals.

    What happened to him: He saved the day, eventually — but after he had already lost his mother to Slade. 

  • Vincent Steelgrave

    Season 3, Episode 1

    What he did: Tried to steal dangerous weapons

    What happened to him: Steelgrave pled guilty and received 15 years in prison.

  • Myron Forest

    Season 3, Episode 5

    What he did: Myron was actually innocent, with Oliver and Felicity believing Myron (the college roommate of Felicity's ex-boyfriend) was responsible for unleashing a virus that was crippling the city. 

    What happened to him: Nothing. Oliver realized the mistake.

  • Oliver Queen

    Season 3, Episode 9

    Note: technically, Ra's Al Ghul didn't say the exact line, telling Oliver, "You failed to protect the city you love."

    What he did: Ra's accused Oliver of failing his city by not bringing him Sara's killer, a move that would necessitate Ra's to exact vengeance upon Starling City. But Oliver lied to Ra's and said he was responsible for Sara's death.

    What happened to him: In a duel, Oliver got a sword through the lung and was kicked off a mountain. He was thought dead, but surived. 

  • Daniel Brickwell

    Season 3, Episode 12

    Note: Laurel and Roy said the famous line together.

    What he did: Brickwell took a the Glades hostage and committed multiple murders. His first murder was Malcolm Merlyn's wife, with her death being the incident that set him on the path to being a supervillain.

    What happened to him: After being beaten to a pulp by Merlyn, he was sent to jail.  

  • Robert Queen

    Season 3, Episode 14

    Note: Robert left Oliver a video message in which he said "I didn't save our city. I failed it."

    What he did: Robert was guilty of cheating on his wife, manslaughter and blackmail.

    What happened to him: He was shipwrecked and ultimately killed himself.
