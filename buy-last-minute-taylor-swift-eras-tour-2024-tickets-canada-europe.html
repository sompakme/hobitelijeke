Where to Stream Taylor Swift: The Eras Tour Concert Film Online

Taylor Swift: The Eras Tour (Taylor’s Version) is now streaming on Disney+. The new version spans three-and-a-half hours and contains the entire concert, plus four acoustic songs not seen in the previous version of the film. The previously-released digital extended version is available to rent for $19.89 on Prime Video, Apple TV and other streaming services.

A star-studded premiere was held on Oc.t 11 in Los Angeles and drew Beyoncé, Adam Sandler, Simu Liu, Julia Garner, Maren Morris and more. Taylor Swift: The Eras Tour was also nominated for a Golden Globe award for best cinematic and box office achievement.

At the Oct. 11 red carpet premiere at The Grove in LA, Swift told the audience, “She shouted out those who worked on the postproduction and editing of the film, saying, “In my mind, it is the perfect capture of what this show was like for me.” Swift also thanked fans “for being so incredibly extra, I appreciate it more than you know. This night is like an extremely core memory for me, so you’re a part of it and I’m so grateful for that.” In one theater, the star sat with her backup singers to watch the movie, as the audience sang and danced along.
