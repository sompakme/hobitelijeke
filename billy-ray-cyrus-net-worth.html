Billy Ray Cyrus Net Worth: A Journey From Achy Breaky Heart to Success

What is Billy Ray Cyrus’s Net Worth and Salary?

Billy Ray Cyrus is a highly successful American singer, songwriter, and actor with a net worth of $20 million. He has gained his wealth majorly from his music career, including album sales, tours, and appearances in various television shows and films.

Early Life and Education

Billy Ray Cyrus was born on August 25, 1961, in Flatwoods, Kentucky, as the son of Ron Cyrus, a politician, and Ruth Ann Casto, a homemaker. He grew up on a farm with four siblings and a large extended family.

Cyrus graduated from Georgetown College in Kentucky with a degree in music theory, before moving to Los Angeles to pursue his career in music.

Career

Cyrus rose to fame with his debut album, “Some Gave All,” which was released in 1992 and topped the Billboard 200 chart. The album gained unprecedented success, selling over 20 million copies worldwide. It featured the famous song “Achy Breaky Heart,” which became a smashing hit and one of the most iconic country music songs of all time.

Since then, Cyrus’s music career has been a mix of ups and downs. He released several commercially successful albums, including “It Won’t Be the Last,” “Trail of Tears,” and “Storm in the Heartland.” In addition to music, he made an impressive acting career with numerous roles in movies like “Mulholland Drive” and the TV series “Doc.”

In recent years, Cyrus’s music career has experienced a surge yet again, thanks to his collaboration with Lil Nas X on the breakout hit, “Old Town Road.” The song topped the charts in various countries and became the longest-running number-one song on the Billboard Hot 100 chart.

Personal Life

Cyrus has been married twice. He tied the knot with Cindy Smith in 1986, and they had two children together, including Miley Cyrus. The couple divorced in 1991.

In 1993, Cyrus married Tish Finley, with whom he has three children. The couple filed for divorce in 2010 but reconciled soon after. They also have an adopted son named Trace Cyrus.

Real Estate

Cyrus has owned several properties throughout his life. He owned a house in Nashville, Tennessee, which he sold in 2020 for $7.5 million. He also owns a sprawling ranch in Franklin, Tennessee.

Legacy

Cyrus’s music style is a blend of country, rock, and pop, making his songs appealing to a wide audience. His contribution to country music has earned him a considerable following, and he has inspired numerous musicians who came after him. His most prominent legacy is the song “Achy Breaky Heart,” which remains a classic country music song even after almost three decades.
