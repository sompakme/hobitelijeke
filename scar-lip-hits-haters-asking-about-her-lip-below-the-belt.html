Scar Lip Hits Haters Asking About Her Lip Below The Belt

 Posted on July 19, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  416 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

NYC rapper Scar Lip recently took to Instagram to respond to haters who wouldn't stop asking about her scar. When asked during a Live, “What actually happened to your lip?” she responded, “What actually happened to that little tiny wee-wee of yours?" She continued, saying, "That’s nothing but a tiny wee-wee of yours. It’s like the n**gas with the tiny wee-wee’s you fall in love with them, right? or Nah, not really.”

Scar Lip shared the origin of her lip scar with XXL, revealing that it's a result of being attacked by her brother. She began, saying, "It was a situation where I was going to a football game and my aunt was like you gotta come back at a certain time and I was like ‘alright whatever…’" "I came back before I was supposed to be in the house, and then my family is acting like they’re not home," Scar Lip explained. "They cut off all the lights to make it seem like they wasn’t home. So I’m knocking on their door because mind you, I’m 16, I live with them I don’t got nowhere else to stay. So I’m knocking on the door waiting for them to open the door and that’s when my little sister came downstairs and she said, ‘Oh you can’t come in,'” she continued.

Scar Lip Shared How She Got Her Scar

The rapper explained, “So I started walking and my brother come outside and he starts asking me for money. I’m like ‘bro I don’t got no money obviously you see I don’t got no money.’ You see the situation I’m in I can’t even get in the crib," she recalled. She revealed, "he started going crazy or whatever and then he punches me." "I wake up and there’s like blood pouring all out of my mouth and stuff," she recounted. She said she remembers, "just screaming, ‘Can somebody help me?'”

Scar Lip recently collaborated with Snoop Dogg, who assisted her in a remix of her song, "This Is New York." The remix, titled, "This Is Cali," features a verse from Snoop. She initially revealed the two of them were planning a collaboration on Instagram, sharing a screenshot of their DMs. When she messaged him asking to collaborate, he replied, “F*ck yes.” She's also previously worked with Jadakiss, Benny The Butcher, Swizz Beatz, and more.
