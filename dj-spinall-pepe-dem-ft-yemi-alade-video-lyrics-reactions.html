The all-time favorite Nigeria DJ has just released a video of his song. DJ Spinall - Pepe Dem has so far bagged a considerable amount of views and encouraging responses on YouTube. What is unique about the video?

DJ Spinall features Yemi Alade, the Nigerian queen whose vocals and dances are the envy of everyone in the music industry. Anytime Yemi appears anywhere, she never disappoints, and this particular video is one good example.

The two artists have teamed up to produce this catchy banger that will make you dance throughout the song. What is more luring in the video are the visual effects and dances.
