  • Recall that Wizkid and Davido shook the music industry and social media after they repeatedly dragged each other online
  • Bright Edafe, in a reaction, claimed Wizkid sang better than Davido while the DMW boss has more hits than the Nigerian Star Boy

The ongoing exchange between Nigerian music stars David Adeleke Davido and Ayo Balogun Wizkid has moved beyond the entertainment industry as the Nigerian police have also gotten involved.

Bright Edafe, the spokesperson of Delta state police command, recently left people talking over his two kobos about Wizkid and Davido.

In a tweet that has since gone viral, Edafe stated that Wizkid was a better singer than Davido. He further stressed that the DMW label boss had more hit songs than Wizkid.

The PPRO, however, urged Davido, Wizkid, and the music industry to take cues from skit makers by learning to support each other.

Sharing a picture of the two stars with Legit.ng collage, Edafe wrote on his page:

"Wizkid sings better and has a better voice but Davido has more hits. They are both great and have done well for themselves and taken Nigeria's music internationally. No need for competition at all. Nigerian musicians should support each other just like our skit makers."
