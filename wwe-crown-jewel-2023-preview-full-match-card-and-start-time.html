WWE Crown Jewel 2023 Preview: Full Match Card, and Start Time

 Posted on July 11, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  492 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

WWE Crown Jewel is upon us, and the event will go down in Crown Jewel is in Mohammed Abdu Arena in Saudi Arabia. The company is bringing another massive card to the Middle East, and we have a full breakdown for the show.

Crown Jewel will start at 1:00 PM EST, and 10:00 AM on the West Coast. There will be an hour-long kickoff show, so fans may want to tune in early, because they do have a match during the kickoff show this time around.

In one of the marquee contests, Cody Rhodes will face Damian Priest, aiming to showcase their wrestling prowess and claim victory. The event also highlights the return of John Cena, who takes on Solo Sikoa, promising a clash of epic proportions.

The WWE Women’s Title will be up for grabs as IYO Sky squares off against Bianca Belair, each determined to emerge victorious and secure the championship. Meanwhile, the WWE Women’s World Title will be contested in a multi-woman match featuring Rhea Ripley, Nia Jax, Zoey Stark, Raquel Rodriguez, and Shayna Baszler. It’s a battle that promises chaos and intrigue. There are also rumors of Kairi Sane’s possible return at the event.

Rey Mysterio puts his WWE United States Title on the line against the formidable Logan Paul, and fans can expect a fierce showdown as they compete for championship glory. Additionally, Seth Rollins and Drew McIntyre will collide in a highly-anticipated bout with the WWE World Heavyweight Title on the line. Only time will tell who is able to hold onto their title, but Logan Paul is an odds-on favorite to win that United States Title match.

The event’s top billing showcases the Undisputed Universal Title match, as Roman Reigns, the reigning champion, squares off against LA Knight. It’s a match that carries immense significance in the wrestling world and will have fans on the edge of their seats.

To kick off the event, Sami Zayn and JD McDonagh will engage in an exciting battle during the Kickoff Show, setting the stage for an action-packed evening of professional wrestling.

With a card filled with championship contests and fierce rivalries, WWE Crown Jewel 2023 is poised to deliver another massive event in Saudi Arabia. You can check out the card below, and we will have full coverage here at Ringside News.

WWE Crown Jewel 2023 Card:

  • Cody Rhodes vs Damian Priest
  • John Cena vs Solo Sikoa
  • IYO Sky vs Bianca Belais – WWE Women’s Title
  • Rhea Ripley vs Nia Jax vs Zoey Stark vs Raquel Rodriguez vs Shayna Baszler – WWE Women’s World Title
  • Rey Mysterio vs Logan Paul – WWE United States Title
  • Seth Rollins vs Drew McIntyre – WWE World Heavyweight Title
  • Roman Reigns vs LA Knight – WWE Undisputed Universal Title
  • Sami Zayn vs JD McDonagh – Kickoff Show
