Popular American rapper, Cardi B, recently reinforced her love for Africa after she tried to learn an important part of the culture, dance.

Cardi B has no doubt been very open about her love for Africa and has even been known to take on traditional names her Nigerian fans blessed her with.

In a new video making the rounds on social media, the Bodak Yellow hitmaker was seen learning to do an African dance.

To get into the theme of the class, the American rapper tied a wrapper made in African print as well as a matching headgear.
