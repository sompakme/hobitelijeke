Remove the GE Dryer’s Beeper from the circuit.

Unplug the dryer from the wall outlet. Locate the wiring schematic on the back of the dryer and carefully examine it to see where the buzzer is located.

Using a screwdriver, remove the screws holding the console together.

Carefully remove the cover from the console.

To disconnect the buzzer, pull one of the wires out of the device.

Replace the screws that hold the console together.
