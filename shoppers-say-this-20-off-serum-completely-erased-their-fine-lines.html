Wrinkle Treatment Drops Retinol Alternative Serum

Retinol is perhaps the preeminent anti-aging ingredient; it fights wrinkles, smooths skin, and refines pores. Unfortunately, the vitamin A-derivative can also pose side effects for first-timers, including redness and irritation as skin becomes acclimated to the ingredient (plus, retinol is not pregnancy-safe). Bakuchiol offers virtually identical anti-aging benefits, sans any of the side effects ascribed to retinol (and it is safe for pregnant users). The plant-based alternative is the star of this serum, which fades fine lines and plumps skin. Also included is probiotic, squalane, an excellent skin-softener. “ This serum gave an instant glow to my perimenopausal skin,” says one reviewer, adding that it “makes [their] skin look so healthy and youthful,” they “stopped wearing any foundation.”
