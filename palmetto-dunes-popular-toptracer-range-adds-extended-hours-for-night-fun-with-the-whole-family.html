Palmetto Dunes popular Toptracer Range adds extended hours for night fun with the whole family!

 Posted on September 6, 2024  |  4 minutes  |  729 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

Now, guests can enjoy night play at Toptracer Range, seasonally & weather permitting. Guests can also enjoy a wide variety of handheld menu options. Lights must go out at 10 p.m. with no exceptions — this is a town ordinance.

Toptracer incorporates high-speed cameras, coupled with sophisticated computer algorithms, to provide instantaneous ball tracking information to guests using the range. The Palmetto Dunes Toptracer Range facility features 14 hitting bays, plus a VIP area all with high-speed cameras, TV monitors, technology and games. Casual seating is available. Each bay can host up to four players. The VIP area is recommended for 8-10 people.

Guests can get their launch, speed, curve and other data analysis — all delivered in real time. At Toptracer Range you can enjoy playing iconic courses or taking aim at virtual targets, getting instant feedback with each shot. There are eight experience options, including the recently added Toptracer30:

  • TOPTRACER30 — Toptracer30 consists of 30 shots: nine tee shots and 21 approach shots. At the end of the game, an estimated performance handicap, a total game score, and  personalized insights are generated that you can use to inform your next practice session.

  • VIRTUAL GOLF — Play some of the world’s greatest golf courses right from your own bay. Individuals and groups can play a full 18 holes without ever having to leave the range. Pebble Beach and Spyglass Hill on California’s Monterey Peninsula are two of the courses available.

  • CLOSEST TO THE PIN — A little friendly competition to get some excitement going in the bay. This game allows friends to compete, while working on their swing.

  • GO FISH — Play individually or as a team to catch each of the 24 different sea creatures and as many stars as possible.

  • APPROACH CHALLENGE — Golfers can fine-tune their iron play in this game of nine approach shots on their choice of three legendary courses.

  • POINTS GAME — This game allows friends to compete for points based on distance from the target and is ideal for people who enjoy a friendly challenge. Guests can even add In-Air bonus rings for an extra challenge.

  • WARM UP — Always be sure to warm up your golf muscles and stretch properly.

  • MY PRACTICE — Dig deeper into the golf bag and learn the nuances of each type of club. This mode also allows golfers to store their stats on the Toptracer Range app.

The Toptracer Range experience at Palmetto Dunes is enhanced with cold drinks and scrumptious food. Food and beverages are available for purchase and delivery to your bay from our bay hosts. There is a range menu available featuring handheld options including nachos, chips and guacamole, chicken tenders, fried pickles, wings, pizzas, warm pretzel bites and hummus. Soft drinks, beer, wine and mixed drinks are also available on the range.

At Palmetto Dunes’ Robert Trent Jones practice facility, the main focus remains meeting the needs of golfers on the tee sheet to warm up before their round. The Toptracer Range features and games are always turned on and you are welcome to use it or not. By making a reservation for Toptracer Range you ensure a space is available for you. The cost is $44 for one-hour bay rental starting at 5 p.m. (up to 4 players per bay – price includes 100 balls), with a $20 per hour add on, $20 for additional 100 balls. Advanced reservations are strongly encouraged. The Arthur Hills Course driving range is available to those just wanting to hit balls without Toptracer.

The Palmetto Dunes Toptracer Range is perfect for your next event or gathering where getting together is always a great time. Treat your guests to Toptracer’s fun and flavor. Host your next birthday, bachelor party, corporate outing, fundraising event, virtual golf tournament or just a casual get together. Palmetto Dunes will make the planning simple and the party unforgettable. You can select your dates and reserve Toptracer bays. Plan a menu with the resort’s Dunes Catering & Events team, choose your beverages and receive a dedicated event planner to ensure a stress-free event.
