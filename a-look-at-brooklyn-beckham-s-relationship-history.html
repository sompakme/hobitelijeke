A Look At Brooklyn Beckham's Relationship History

 Posted on August 18, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  240 words  |  Larita Shotwell

Actors, models, singers, and now Playboy bunnies: is there anyone Brooklyn Beckham hasn't dated? After being spotted making out at a tattoo parlor with Playboy model Lexi Wood in 2018, rumors began to swirl that this fling may have overlapped with Beckham's relationship with Chloë Moretz.

Although it takes two to tango, it was, unfortunately, Wood who was subjected to vile online abuse following the allegations. As the Evening Standard reports, fans of Moretz targeted Wood, claiming that she was responsible for "Breaking up true love." To make matters even shadier, Beckham liked an Instagram post from a fan page, which showed a photo of him crying while being comforted by his mom above the caption "Chloe's fans are attacking Brooklyn Beckham," as per Elle.

Beckham went shirtless on Instagram to show off his new tattoo of a striking brunette, which bore an uncanny resemblance to Wood. W Magazine published a screenshot, showing that Wood liked the post. Not just that, but she commented a tongue-out emoji, suggesting that she was thirsty for Becks. As per Capital FM, Wood hinted at the newfound romance by posting a video on Instagram in which she appeared to mimic the aforementioned tongue out emoji, as the Dua Lipa song "One Kiss" played, which may have been viewed as salt in the wound by Team Moretz. It's unclear when Wood and Becks split, but there's little doubt it was nothing more than a fling.
