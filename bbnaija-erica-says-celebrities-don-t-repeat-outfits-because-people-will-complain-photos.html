Celebrities are held to a higher standard by their fans and followers and it influences the way they live their lives. This is not in any way different for Nigerian celebrities.

Recently, BBNaija star Erica Nlewedim held a question and answer session for her fans and she responded to all they asked her.

A fan asked the Lockdown star why celebrities do not repeat clothes and she stated that their fans and followers are the reason.

According to Erica, people will complain if they see a celebrity repeating one outfit. However, she noted that it does not apply to her.

Another follower then asked the reality star to share some of her funny photos and Erica posted some from years ago.

The BBNaija star made a collage of three throwback photos that she seemed to like.

The first photo showed her in a green graduation gown. The young woman posed for a photo with the gown during her convocation ceremony in school.

In the second photo, she was spotted in a beautiful pink dress as her black wig fell on her shoulders. According to her, it was when she contested for the Most Beautiful Girl in Nigeria.

The third one showed her in her National Youth Service Corps uniform. The photo was taken when she served her fatherland after school.

Despite the fact that the photos were taken at different times, Erica had a beautiful smile plastered on her face in all of them.
