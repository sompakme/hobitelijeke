Sweden Officially Declares Sex As Sport, Championship Next Week!

 Posted on August 26, 2024  |  1 minutes  |  188 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

Sweden officially declared sex as a sport and the country also announced to organise a championship in it.

In a first of its kind, the sex competition will have people participate in it and engage in sex sessions which can go up to six hours daily. A panel of judges will decide the winners while the audience will also get to influence the final decisions.

The European Sex Championship will start on June 8 and continue over a period of six weeks with participants engaging in sexual activities from 45 minutes to 1 hour daily.

The audience will notice several aspects of the activities and the final decision on the winners will be concluded after looking at the factors like knowledge about sex, endurance level, and of course chemistry.

The knowledge of Sanskrit scripture on sex, Kamasutra also will play a role in choosing the winners.

In addition, the championship can have participants from different sexual orientations. These ‘Sex Championships’ welcomed mixed reactions on social media as some started supporting the removal of inhibitions about sex while others called it weird.
