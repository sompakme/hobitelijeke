Renowned R&B star, Trey Songz, born Tremaine Aldon Neverson, has been arrested after getting into an altercation with a police officer at the AFC Championship Game.

Trey attended the NFL match held on Sunday, January 24, between the Kansas City Chiefs and the Buffalo Bills at Arrowhead Stadium in Kansas City, Missouri, but things turned out ugly soon after.

According to TMZ, the rapper was filmed getting into a physical altercation with a police officer after he had asked fans, seated a few rows behind him, that were heckling at him to be quiet.

It was alleged that the singer was caught off guard, defended himself, and even connected a punch with the officer before being pinned to a seat.
