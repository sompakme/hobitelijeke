It is often advised to celebrate other people’s success to attract good things and it appears that is exactly what Nollywood actress, Mercy Aigbe, is doing.

Just recently, the movie star took to her page on Instagram with a celebratory post dedicated to her friend and celebrity designer, Abike Domina.

Apparently, the designer splashed several millions of naira on a brand new Range Rover Velar automobile.

Mercy posted a picture of her friend posing with the new car as she congratulated her for the luxury car purchase.

The actress in her caption noted that her ‘girls’ are ballers. She also said that congratulatory messages are the only valid conversations she wants to have in 2021.
