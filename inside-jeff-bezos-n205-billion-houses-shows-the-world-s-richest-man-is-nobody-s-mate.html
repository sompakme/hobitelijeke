3. Washington DC (Textile Museum, 2320-2330 S Street, Kalorama - $23 million)

In 2016, Bezos paid $23 million (N9,443,110,000) for the former Textile Museum in Washington’s upscale Kalorama neighbourhood.

He was said to have renovated it with $12 million (N4,926,840,000). The museum was originally built in 1914.

4. Home across the street (2325 S Street NW - $5 million)

According to Washingtonian, the Amazon boss also bought the 4,800-square-foot home across the street for $5 million (N2,052,850,000) in January 2020.

It's a four-bedroom home that was built in 1951. Its features include five full bathrooms and two powder rooms.

Other features include vaulted ceilings, detailed crown molding, carved marble fireplaces and distressed hardwood floors, among others.
