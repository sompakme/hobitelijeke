Teen Icon In Classic Films

Robert Downey Jr and Anthony Michael Hall on set of the film 'Johnny Be Good', 1988. (Photo by Orion/Getty Images)

Anthony Michael Hall's breakthrough came with his roles in the classic John Hughes films that have become cultural touchstones. Starting with National Lampoon’s Vacation, Hall's portrayal of Rusty Griswold introduced him to audiences as a charismatic young actor. His subsequent roles in Sixteen Candles, The Breakfast Club, and Weird Science solidified his place as a quintessential figure in '80s cinema. These films highlighted his knack for capturing the nuances of adolescent angst and allowed him to showcase a range of emotions, from comedic timing to poignant vulnerability.
