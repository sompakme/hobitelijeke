The Best Rituals For Attracting Good Luck

 Posted on September 26, 2024  |  8 minutes  |  1595 words  |  Aldo Pusey

Are you feeling stuck under a cloud of bad luck lately? You can turn things around with simple rituals to attract good luck.

Whether you know it or not, you’re always manifesting. Rituals help you focus your energies toward manifesting what you want. For instance, you can channel your body’s vibrational frequency to manifest good luck.

You raise your energy with your emotion, thoughts, and actions. They’re all essential to ritual work. Let’s dive into the best rituals for good luck so you can make sure you’re manifesting positivity and success.

Can Luck Be Attracted?

Rituals for good luck can help shift your energy and make you more intentional about your actions. Where attention goes, energy flows.

For example, if you believe you can make a certain amount of money, you’re more likely to take the right actions to make it happen. Alternatively, you may not even take the first step if you don’t believe you can achieve a big goal.

Additionally, optimistic people tend to attract more in every area of life, like business, romance, and personal goals. Some people seem to be born with good luck. However, you can start attracting good luck too.

Signs You Have a Bad Luck

The universe will attempt to teach you the same lesson until you learn from it. So, you’ve likely noticed some negative patterns if you’re experiencing bad luck. Here are some signs you have bad luck and what to do next.

You’re Feeling Anxious and Unbalanced

The first sign you have bad luck is if you often feel unbalanced, are struggling with anxiety, or both. Additionally, bad luck is likely to add to these negative feelings once circumstances seem to go the opposite way you want them to constantly; it can feel like you’re forever doomed to bad luck.

It can quickly turn into an endless loop of having bad luck and feeling anxious. When this happens, you must stop it in its tracks. Don’t let the past bog you down; don’t anxiously obsess about the future.

Negative energy attracts negative energy. So even when bad things happen, it’s essential to flip the script and attract new positive experiences.

You’re Constantly Losing Things

If you’re constantly losing things, this can signify lousy luck. Plus, losing things may add to anxious and unbalanced feelings.

Losing things may be a sign to stop and pay attention. The universe is trying to teach you something, so consider what you’re losing to determine its meaning.

For example, if you lose your car keys, the universe may attempt to tell you to slow down and relax. Losing things may also signify that you’ve been careless lately and the universe is guiding you to be more intentional.

You’re Prone to Injuries

If you’re feeling unlucky, it may be due to all the injuries you’ve had lately. Like losing things, the universe may be communicating with you.

Pain is meant to tell us something is wrong and that we must change to feel better. So, if you have tension, for example, the universe guides you to solve a stressful issue or change your direction.

Alternatively, your pain may be guiding you to something greater. For example, if you sprain your ankle, the universe may try to get you to the doctor.

Others are Avoiding You

Negative energy is palpable. Have you ever been in a room where one negative person brings the entire vibe of the room down?

We all have moments where we aren’t feeling the greatest emotionally. However, it’s vital to move through life with intention and not let the bad times get us down. Otherwise, all we’ll do is attract more negativity.

If people are distancing themselves from you, let them for now. Wait until you get your energy back on track before you contact them. Once they see you’re beaming with positive energy, they won’t be able to help but want to be around you.

Rituals for Bringing Good Luck Into Your Life

Once you realize you’ve been attracting bad luck, it’s time to start bringing good into your life. Here are some rituals to attract more positivity, abundance, and happiness.

Buy a Lucky Plant

Bringing an element of the Earth is an excellent way to raise your vibration and attract good luck. Consider getting a bamboo plant or money tree, though any plant will do as long as you set the intention.

Take great care of your new plant by researching its specific needs. Use this time as an abundance ritual when caring for your plant by watering, fertilizing, repotting, etc.

Think of what you’re trying to attract while caring for your plant. When you begin to notice positive experiences, thank your plant for repaying you for the care you gave it.

Recite Abundance Affirmations

Abundance affirmations are one of the best ways to manifest good luck. Specifically, focus on all that you’re grateful for in the present moment. Gratitude for what you have encourages more blessings to follow.

After all, if you aren’t grateful for all you’re currently blessed with, why would you be thankful for more? Try to feel deeply about everything you feel blessed to have.

For example, if you’re reading this, you have electricity and a device that instantly lets you have this information. The more you practice gratitude, the more you’ll see the endless things, people, and circumstances you must be grateful for.

Practice Candle Magic

Purchase a prosperity candle at a local or online spiritual store. Before you light it, list the abundance you’re trying to manifest in all areas of life.

As you light the candle, say your intentions out loud. Stare at the flame and imagine all the blessings coming as if you ordered them and they’re out for delivery.

Place some crystals near it that align with what you’re trying to manifest to take things up a notch. Extinguish the flame with a candle snuffer to avoid “blowing” the desires away.

Sprinkle Cinnamon and Salt Near Front Door

Several spiritual groups view cinnamon as a healing agent that obtains vibrational qualities that attract abundance to those who use it. No wonder Christmastime feels so magical.

Sprinkling a bit of cinnamon at the front door can set the intention to attract good fortune into your home. As you do so, say your intentions out loud.

Additionally, you can add salt to ward off evil spirits and negative energy, making your cinnamon even more potent.

Perform a Bath Ritual

Bath rituals are one of the most popular forms of manifesting, and they’ve been performed for centuries across many cultures.

Once you fill your bath, add Epsom salt, a few drops of bergamot essential oil, and a tablespoon of milk. Add rosemary leaves and roses to the tub using a mesh bag to avoid a post-bath mess.

While you bathe, reach out to your spirit guides and meditate on what you desire. Use this time to relax into the feeling that what you want is already yours and on its way to you.

Clean Your Space With a Sage

Burning sage, or smudging, is one of the oldest cleansing methods. For example, new homeowners often burn sage to remove unwanted spirits or negative energy from a room.

If you’ve been having bad luck, remove the negative frequency by lighting a bundle of sage and cleansing your home. Keep your windows open; otherwise, the negative energy won’t have anywhere to escape.

A study shows that burning sage can remove over 94% of bacteria in the air.

Things to Keep in Mind While Performing Good Luck Rituals

Here are a few things to remember while performing good luck rituals to get the most out of them.

Perform Rituals Only When You’re Calmed and Relaxed

Energy is powerful, so you want to be in a good headspace when performing some rituals. Start with gratitude, and get into the feeling of being thankful for what you have and excited for what’s coming.

If you have anxious energy while performing your rituals, it will bleed into your spells. Then, this negative energy will affect the results you’re trying to achieve.

Set a Date For Rituals

Although some rituals, like gratitude, work well as a daily practice, it’s best to set a date for other rituals. Consider doing good luck rituals on new and full moons to strengthen the power.

Additionally, choose the right location to perform your rituals. Entrances can act like cosmic portals, so it’s a good idea to do rituals for good luck near doors, windows, etc.

Think Positive Thoughts

Positivity attracts more positivity, so keep your thoughts positive. If a negative thought seeps in, observe it and allow it to flow through you.

You’ll notice when negative thoughts pop up as you’re more intentional. Use this as an opportunity to reframe it into something more positive.

Focus on Specific Goals

What do you want? Are you searching for love? A raise? A new opportunity?

Get intentional about your desires, and they’re more likely to appear.

How Often to Perform Good Luck Rituals?

Good luck rituals work no matter when you perform them, as the intention and positive energy behind them are essential.

However, doing them at dawn or dusk and a full moon or a new moon can add extra power to your manifesting.

Conclusion/Summary

You really can achieve everything you want through manifestation. Although manifestation is about attracting, it’s vital to take inspired action to make it happen. Use good luck rituals to harness positive energy, put it out into the universe, and watch it deliver more blessings to you.
