Kojis

Credit: Courtesy

Antique purveyor Kojis has one of the most beautiful collections of one-off pieces, and this Art Deco-inspired sapphire and diamond gold ring proves why.

Dinny Hall

Credit: Courtesy

Look to Dinny Hall for beautifully classic designs. This five Sapphire ring is part of the company's Elyhara collection which is a modern reinterpretation of early Georgian gem-set jewellery.

Cartier

Credit: Courtesy

You can never go wrong with Cartier. This majestic band is set with 14 diamonds and 14 sapphires.

Fred Leighton

Drawing on vintage design, this show-stopper from Fred Leighton is crafted from white gold and features an emerald-cut diamond flanked by rich blue sapphires.

Jessica McCormack

Credit: Courtesy

Love is in the air and there's no denying it with Jessica McCormack's heart-shaped sapphire ring.

Tiffany & Co

Credit: Courtesy

A true sparkler, this Tiffany design features a mesmerising round sapphire cushioned by endless diamonds.

Polly Wales

If you're asking someone who would prefer a more untraditional look, Polly Wales' confetti ring is perfect. Made from 18ct gold, it features both black and white sapphires.

Harry Winston

Credit: Courtesy

Harry Winston's jewellery is some of the most beautiful in the world and no two pieces are ever the same. This cushion-cut sapphire design is the ring of dreams.

Suzanne Kalan
