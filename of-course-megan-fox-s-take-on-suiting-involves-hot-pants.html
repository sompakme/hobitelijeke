Of Course Megan Fox's Take on Suiting Involves Hot Pants

 Posted on August 14, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  246 words  |  Aldo Pusey

Thanks to her press tour for Pretty Boys Are Poisonous in New York City, Megan Fox is fueling our fall mood boards one bold look at a time. After looking like a cup of hot cocoa (emphasis on the word hot) in a brown leather skirt and top earlier this week, the actress schooled us with a sexy take on classic suiting.

On Wednesday, Fox was spotted arriving at the Drew Barrymore Show while wearing another all-brown ensemble that was comprised of a cropped tailored blazer, a camel-colored trench coat draped around her shoulders, and the tiniest matching hot pants. Never missing an opportunity to accessorize, she coordinated her on-trend seasonal crimson manicure with a pair of Paris Texas rouge noir pumps, matching sunglasses, and sheer tights.

Beauty-wise, she styled her fiery red bob with flipped-out ends and baby bangs, which she pinned back with baby blue butterfly clips. She opted for a simple makeup look that included bold brows, rosy cheeks, and a glossy pout.

Megan has officially entered her author era — and she's dressing the part. While promoting her book (a collection of poems about relationships) on Tuesday, Megan was photographed sporting a second monochromatic brown look that featured a chocolate-colored tank tucked into a matching leather maxi skirt with a flared bottom. Keeping the accessories to a minimum, she slipped into a pair of black patent-leather slouchy stiletto boots that peeked out from underneath the ankle-length hem.
