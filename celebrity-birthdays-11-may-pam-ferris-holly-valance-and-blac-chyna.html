Celebrity birthdays 11 May: Pam Ferris, Holly Valance and Blac Chyna

 Posted on July 10, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  292 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

Having a birthday on 11 May is extra special this year as it falls on a Friday – making for a long weekend of celebrating ahead! Famous faces set to mark their big day today include Call the Midwife star Pam Ferris, singer Holly Valance and model Blac Chyna. Now another year older, and another year wiser, we take a look at what these three stars have been up to over the past year, and we wish them, and everyone else with a birthday on this day, many happy returns.

Pam Ferris turns 70

It's a milestone birthday for legendary actress Pam Ferris as she turns 70 on Friday. The star – who is known for roles including Ms Trunchbull in the 1996 film Matilda, and Aunt Marge in Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban – will no doubt be spending her special day with husband, actor Roger Frost, and her beloved dogs, Stanley and Elsie.

Holly Valance turns 35

This birthday is set to be extra special for Holly Valance, as it will be the first one with her baby daughter Nova, who was born in September 2017, joining older sister Luka, three. The former Neighbours star is sure to be spoilt on her special day by husband Nick and her young daughters – and we are sure that Luka will be on hand to help her with the candles on her cake!

Blac Chyna turns 30

Blac Chyna is set to enter her thirties as she approaches the big 3-0 on Friday, and is sure to have a lovely day with her two children, King, five, and Dream, one, having hit the headlines of late with pregnancy rumours of her third pregnancy with 18-year-old boyfriend YBM Almighty Jay.
