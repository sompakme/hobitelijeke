Ancient seeds are items found on Greydwarf brute and nests. They also randomly spawn in Chests.

Where can I find ancient seed in Valheim?

Ancient Seeds are Greydwarf young and can be obtained as a random drop after defeating certain varieties of Greydwarf. Players will need to venture into the Black Forest to find these creatures and eventually summon The Elder.

Where do Ancient Seeds spawn?

The Ancient Seed is an Artifact that can be found by digging up an Artifact Spot in Cindersap Forest or the Mountains (including the Quarry), or obtained inside Fishing Treasure Chests and Artifact Troves. It is also a rare drop from Bugs, Grubs, Cave Flies, Mutant Flies, and Mutant Grubs (0.5%).

How do you burn Ancient Seeds in Valheim?

Once you’ve acquired three Ancient Seeds, head back to the ceremonial site on your map. Approach the fire in the center and add your Ancient Seeds to your hotbar. Throw them into the fire by selecting whichever slot you’ve placed them into. Run to a safe place as The Elder will spawn shortly afterwards.

Ancient Seeds can also be obtained from Ancient Fruit by using a Seed Maker. Whenever the Seed Maker is used with any crop other than Ancient Fruit, there is also a very small chance (0.5%) to obtain 1-3 Ancient Seeds instead of the crop’s seeds.

How do you burn ancient seeds?

Step Three: Obtain Three Ancient Seeds

These are small, tree-like objects radiating purple smoke. The easiest way to destroy these are by running up and hacking at it with your weapon, or firing arrows at it from afar. You can take the hint from The Elder’s runestone and burn it with your torch or fire arrows, as well.

What is the easiest way to beat Elder Valheim?

The key to this fight is to keep The Elder at medium range, avoiding (or elminating) its summoned roots, and staying away from its spiked root attack. Keep a steady barrage of fire arrows on The Elder, as the fire will damage its health over time and keep its health bar slowly draining as you navigate the arena.

What is the safest biome in Valheim?

The Meadows biome is the first biome you will encounter when spawning into the world of Valheim. As its lush grassy meadows, flowing rivers, and rolling hills are home to relatively harmless Greylings, Necks, and Boars, the Meadows biome is by far the safest biome you will experience in Valheim.
