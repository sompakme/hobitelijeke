Jace Norman &amp; Isabela Moner Relationship: Nickelodeon Star Talks Dating Reveal

 Posted on July 19, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  364 words  |  Aldo Pusey

Jace Norman and Isabela Moner announced their relationship with a splash. Remember those kissing photos from earlier this spring? Knowing that fans would be freaking out about the fact that they're dating, you would think that they spent a lot of time figuring out how to drop the big news. Spoiler: They didn't.

Being in a relationship in the public eye has serious ups and downs, but the Henry Danger star says that their romance is a part of his life, and he wanted to share it. However, he was a bit scared to see how the fans would react!

“Honestly, I was a little bit worried about how they would react. They’re my fans and obviously I want them to be happy, but it’s my life and if they don’t like it, there’s nothing I can do," he said in a recent interview.

The Nickelodeon star insists that they didn't even plan a major reveal, and he didn't want to hide their relationship to please other people.

“It’s not like there was this strategic decision we made, or a major discussion, it’s just a normal thing. I think everyone who follows me wants to know more about my life, and so it’s not something I am going to hide just because some people may get upset," he said.

Jace says that he doesn't even consider his relationship with Isabela a super-personal, private thing — she's just an important part of life, and that's reflected on his social media accounts.

“I know some people are more private on Instagram, but it’s my life and I want to share it and be real. I don’t want to hide it or anything, unless it’s super personal. My relationship isn’t like a personal thing. Its just a thing in my life right now. I have friends and post pics with them and I have a girlfriend and will post pictures with her.”

And don't we know it!
