Early Life and Career Beginnings

Jeff Garlin was born and raised in Chicago, Illinois. He began his career in stand-up comedy and later moved to Los Angeles to pursue acting. His first significant role was in the film “RoboCop 3,” and he later landed roles in popular TV shows like “Mad About You” and “Everybody Loves Raymond.”

Breakthrough with ‘Curb Your Enthusiasm’

Garlin’s breakthrough came when he was cast as Jeff Greene in the HBO series “Curb Your Enthusiasm.” His performance in the show earned him several award nominations and significantly increased his popularity and net worth.

Success with ‘The Goldbergs’

Garlin’s success continued with his role as Murray Goldberg in the ABC sitcom “The Goldbergs.” The show has been a hit, further boosting Garlin’s fame and fortune.

Other Ventures

Besides acting, Garlin has also ventured into directing, producing, and writing. He directed, produced, and starred in the film “I Want Someone to Eat Cheese With” and has written several books, including “My Footprint: Carrying the Weight of the World.”
