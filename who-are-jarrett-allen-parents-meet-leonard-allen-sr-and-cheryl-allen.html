Who are Jarrett Allen Parents? Meet Leonard Allen Sr. and Cheryl Allen

 Posted on August 13, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  531 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

Jarrett Allen was drafted by the Brooklyn Nets with the 22nd overall pick in the 2017 NBA Draft. After four seasons in Brooklyn, Allen was traded to the Cleveland Cavaliers in 2021 as part of a four player deal with the Houston Rockets that saw James Harden joining the Nets. Known for his defense and strong finishing ability around the rim, the center took part in his first All-Star game in 2022, as a replacement for James Harden on Team LeBron.

In the 2022/23 season, Allen helped the Cavs (51-31) advance to the playoffs for the first time in five seasons. In 68 regular season games, he averaged 14.3 points, 9.8 rebounds, 1.7 assists and 1.2 blocks per game. He, along with Evan Mobley, led the Cavs from a winning percentage of .306 and the sixth worst defense in the league to a winning percentage of .537 and having the fifth best defense in the league.

Jarrett Allen has also participated in the FIBA Americas U18 Championship in Chile in 2016, where he won the gold for Team USA.

Jarrett Allen’s Parents

Leonard Allen Sr. and Cheryl Allen are Jarrett Allen’s parents. He was born in San Diego, California, but his parents relocated to Round Rock, Texas when he was eight years old.

Who is Jarrett Allen’s Mother?

Cheryl Allen is the mother of Jarett Allen. Despite not being his agent, she takes all important decisions regarding her son’s life. An instance of this was when her son was searching for a house in Brooklyn, his mother suggested that the building he lived in must have a permanent doorman. Nevertheless, Allen purchased a house with two bedrooms so that his parents can stay comfortably when they visit him.

Who is Jarrett Allen’s Father?

Leonard Allen Sr. is the father of Jarrett Allen. He is a former basketball player who was drafted by the Dallas Mavericks in 1985. He now works as a manager at Dell.

Allen credits his father as the kind of person who would always advise and help him in serious situations. At the same time, he would also ask his son to take responsibility for his actions if he caused mischief and got into trouble in school.

What Nationality are Jarett Allen’s Parents?

Both Leonard Allen Sr. and Cheryl Allen are American nationals. They are originally from San Diego, California but now reside in Round Rock, Texas. Nevertheless, they are African-Americans in ethnicity.
