Heidi Klum and Daughter Leni Pose in Lingerie Together for New Photo Shoot

 Posted on August 11, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  389 words  |  Aldo Pusey

Heidi Klum and Leni Klum are continuing to take over the fashion industry as a mother-daughter duo.

For their latest fashion project together, the pair starred in a campaign for Italian lingerie brand Intimissimi. In photos and videos from the photo shoot, Heidi, 49, and Leni, 18, are seen posing, embracing, and laughing in different sets of lingerie.

In one shot, used as an ad for the brand, Heidi wears a semi-sheer black bra and thong embroidered with white flowers. Next to her, Leni poses in a black scalloped bra with a geometric pattern and matching high-waisted panties. Over the set, Leni wears a silky ivory robe. In another photo, Leni wears the same set in white.

"Together with @intimissimiofficial we celebrate women; the love and support between a mother and her daughter; and how confident, beautiful and happy lingerie can make you feel," Heidi captioned an Instagram post featuring one of the shots of her and her daughter.

In the video for the campaign, creative-directed by Thomas Hayo, Heidi and Leni dance around in their lingerie and sing along to the words of an opera-like song in Italian, written for the occasion, which plays in the background.
