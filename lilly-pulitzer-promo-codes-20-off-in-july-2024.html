Does Lilly Pulitzer have free shipping?

Yes. Get free ground shipping on all your orders at Lilly Pulitzer. Once shipping has been confirmed, your order will take 3-5 working days to arrive. If you need your order quicker, then 2-day and next-day shipping options are available for an additional cost.  

Can I get a 20% off discount at Lilly Pulitzer?

Yes, sign up for the Lilly Pulitzer email newsletter to get 20% off your first purchase. Simply go to the Lilly Pulitzer website and scroll to the email sign-up box, where you can type your preferred email address.  

When will Lilly Pulitzer have a sale?

There is no dedicated sales section, but the brand is known for its surprise sales called ‘Sunshine sales’. Generally, these take place twice a year with around 60% off selected items, so be sure to sign up for the newsletter for updates. If you miss the ‘Sunshine Sales’, there are always Black Friday and Cyber Monday sales. 

Does Lilly Pulitzer do a birthday discount?

Yes. Sign up for the Lilly Pulitzer loyalty program to get a birthday coupon each year. You will be sent a Lilly Pulitzer 15% off coupon for a single full-priced purchase on your birth month each year. FYI: you will need to save your birth date to your loyalty account before the first day of your birthday month.  

What is the returns policy at Lilly Pulitzer?

Lilly Pulitzer will accept any return within 90 days of purchase. If you return your items within 15 days then returns are free, otherwise returns will cost $7. Your items should be returned in their original condition with any tags or hygiene labels still attached. Any promotional items should also be returned.
